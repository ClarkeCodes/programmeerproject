Depression and Suicide Around the World

Countries that have the highest rates of depression don't necessarily have the highest rates of suicide. If you hover over the map you can see the rate of depression in % of the population in a country, as well as the rate of suicide per 100,000 people in that country. Clicking on the map will take you to the next screen, where you can explore trends and demographics in suicide.
